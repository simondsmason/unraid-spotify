How to get your credentials:

  1. Go to Spotify Developer Dashboard
  2. Click "Create App" or select an existing app
  3. Copy your Client ID and Client Secret
  4. Click "Edit Settings"
  5. Under "Redirect URIs", add: http://localhost:8081/callback
  6. If accessing from network, also add: http://<your-ip>:8081/callback
  7. Click "Add" and "Save"
  8. Paste the credentials in the form above
